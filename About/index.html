About Me

I'm currently a third-year B.S. Software Engineering student with a deep passion for technology and problem-solving. My journey in software development began in high school when I built my first website, and I've been hooked ever since.

Throughout my academic career, I've developed a strong foundation in computer science fundamentals, data structures, algorithms, and software design patterns. I'm particularly interested in web development, machine learning, and building scalable systems that make a real-world impact.

I believe in continuous learning and staying current with emerging technologies. Whether it's exploring a new framework, contributing to open-source projects, or collaborating with teammates on challenging problems, I'm always eager to expand my skills and take on new challenges.

Beyond coding, I enjoy mentoring fellow students, participating in hackathons, and giving back to the developer community. I'm actively seeking internship opportunities where I can apply my knowledge, learn from experienced professionals, and contribute to innovative projects.

When I'm not at my computer, you can find me reading tech blogs, playing basketball, or experimenting with new coffee brewing techniques. I'm excited about what the future holds and look forward to making meaningful contributions to the tech industry.
